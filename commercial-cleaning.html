Service Overview

Our Commercial Cleaning service is designed to meet the unique needs of businesses across London. We understand that a clean workplace is essential for productivity, employee well-being, and creating positive first impressions with clients and visitors.

Whether you manage a small office, a large corporate building, retail space, or medical facility, our experienced team delivers consistent, high-quality cleaning services tailored to your business requirements. We work around your schedule, offering flexible cleaning times including after-hours, early morning, or weekend services to minimize disruption to your operations.

Our commercial cleaning service goes beyond basic tidying. We use industry-leading equipment and eco-friendly cleaning products to ensure thorough sanitization and hygiene. From daily maintenance cleaning to deep periodic cleans, we create customized cleaning plans that align with your budget and standards.

All our commercial cleaners are fully vetted, trained, and insured. We maintain strict quality control standards and provide regular supervision to ensure consistent excellence. Your business deserves a cleaning partner you can trust.

What's Included

Office Areas

  • Dust & wipe all desks & surfaces
  • Vacuum all carpets & rugs
  • Empty & sanitize waste bins
  • Clean & disinfect door handles
  • Wipe down computer keyboards & phones
  • Clean glass partitions & windows
  • Dust window sills & blinds
  • Straighten chairs & meeting rooms

Washrooms & Facilities

  • Deep clean & sanitize toilets
  • Clean & polish sinks & taps
  • Refill soap & paper dispensers
  • Mop & disinfect floors
  • Clean mirrors & glass surfaces
  • Empty sanitary bins
  • Wipe & disinfect all surfaces
  • Remove limescale & stains

Kitchen & Break Rooms

  • Clean & sanitize countertops
  • Wipe down appliances (exterior)
  • Clean microwave inside & out
  • Empty & clean sink area
  • Wipe tables & chairs
  • Empty & replace bin liners
  • Sweep & mop floors
  • Wipe cupboard doors & handles

Reception & Common Areas

  • Dust & polish reception desk
  • Vacuum & clean all seating
  • Clean entrance doors & glass
  • Mop hard floor surfaces
  • Dust light fixtures & decor
  • Clean stairways & handrails
  • Empty & sanitize bins
  • Spot clean walls & baseboards
