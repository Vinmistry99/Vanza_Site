Service Overview

Our Deep Cleaning service is a meticulous, intensive process designed to reach the dirt and grime that regular cleaning often misses. We go beyond surface-level tidying to ensure every nook and cranny of your home is thoroughly sanitized and refreshed.

Using professional-grade equipment and eco-friendly products, our experienced team tackles stubborn stains, built-up grease, and hidden dust. This service is ideal for a seasonal reset, preparing a home for a special event, or establishing a baseline of cleanliness before beginning a recurring service.

We pride ourselves on our attention to detail, ensuring that high-touch areas, baseboards, interior windows, and inside appliances are left sparkling clean.

What's Included

Kitchen

  • Inside/outside all appliances
  • Deep clean of oven and extractor
  • Inside cabinets and drawers
  • Descale sink and polish taps
  • Degrease backsplashes
  • Skirting boards and door frames

Bathrooms

  • Intensive limescale removal
  • Scrub and sanitize tiles/grout
  • Toilet deep clean inside/out
  • Clean mirrors and glass
  • Inside vanity and cabinets
  • Extractor fan dusting

Living Areas & Bedrooms

  • Dusting all surfaces and decor
  • Interior windows and sills
  • Vacuum upholstery and under cushions
  • Skirting boards and light switches
  • Inside wardrobes (if empty)
  • Thorough vacuum and mop of floors
