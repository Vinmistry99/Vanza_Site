Common questions

How will I receive my £20?
We can pay by bank transfer, PayPal, or another agreed method — you choose what works best.
When will I get paid?
After your referral’s cleaning is completed and they are satisfied, we process payment within 3–5 working days.
Can I refer multiple people?
Yes — there is no limit. Each qualifying new client who completes a clean earns you £20.
Does my referral need to be a first-time customer?
Yes. The reward applies when they are a new customer who has not used our services before.
Do referrals expire?
No — as long as they mention your name when they first book, you qualify for the reward when that clean completes.
Can I track my referrals?
We can keep you updated on status and notify you when each referral books and completes their service — ask when you get in touch.
