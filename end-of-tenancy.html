Service Overview

Our End of Tenancy Cleaning service is specifically designed to meet the exacting standards of landlords, letting agents, and inventory clerks. We understand that your deposit depends on leaving the property in pristine condition, and we're committed to helping you achieve that.

This comprehensive service covers every inch of the property, from ceiling to floor, including all fixtures, fittings, and appliances. Our experienced team follows a detailed checklist aligned with industry standards, ensuring nothing is overlooked during the final inspection.

We use professional-grade equipment and eco-friendly cleaning products to tackle stubborn stains, limescale, grease, and accumulated dirt. Our goal is to return the property to its original condition, giving you peace of mind as you transition to your new home.

All our End of Tenancy cleans come with a 48-hour guarantee. If your landlord or agent identifies any issues during inspection, we'll return to rectify them at no additional cost.

What's Included

Kitchen

  • Deep clean oven, hob & extractor
  • Degrease all surfaces & tiles
  • Clean inside/outside all appliances
  • Empty & clean all cupboards/drawers
  • Descale sink, taps & drain
  • Clean fridge/freezer inside & out
  • Wipe down all doors & handles
  • Clean skirting boards & switches

Bathrooms

  • Remove all limescale & staining
  • Deep clean toilet inside & out
  • Scrub tiles, grout & sealant
  • Polish all chrome & fixtures
  • Clean shower screen/curtain
  • Empty & clean all cabinets
  • Clean mirrors & glass surfaces
  • Clean extractor fan & vents

Living Areas & Bedrooms

  • Dust all surfaces & furniture
  • Clean interior windows & frames
  • Vacuum/clean all upholstery
  • Empty & wipe all wardrobes
  • Clean skirting boards thoroughly
  • Wipe all light switches & sockets
  • Clean radiators front & back
  • Vacuum & mop all floors

Hallways & General

  • Clean all doors & door frames
  • Polish all handles & hardware
  • Dust light fittings & lampshades
  • Clean behind & under furniture
  • Cobweb removal from ceilings
  • Vacuum stairs & landings
