The Vanza Difference

Vanza was born out of frustration with the inconsistent, unreliable cleaning services prevalent in London. We envisioned a service that matched the premium quality of the homes and businesses it cared for—a brand built on trust, meticulous attention to detail, and a seamless customer experience.

Today, we are proud to be London's trusted partner for residential and commercial cleaning. From deep cleans in Mayfair to daily office maintenance in the City, our rigorous standards remain the same.

Our Vetted Cleaners Process

We hire only the top 1% of applicants. Our rigorous 4-step vetting process ensures that every Vanza professional is trustworthy, experienced, and dedicated to our high standards.

1

Background Checks

Comprehensive identity verification, right-to-work checks, and thorough reference follow-ups for every candidate.

2

In-Person Interviews

Detailed behavioral and situational interviews to assess professionalism, communication, and problem-solving skills.

3

Skills Assessment

Practical cleaning tests in simulated environments to guarantee expertise in various cleaning techniques and surfaces.

4

Vanza Academy

Intensive onboarding covering our specific quality standards, eco-friendly product usage, and customer service protocols.

How your clean works

From booking to follow-up, this is the journey every client can expect—clear steps, open communication, and no surprises.

1

Book & confirm

Choose your service, property size, and preferred time. You receive a clear quote and a confirmed booking with pricing agreed upfront.

2

Before we arrive

Reminders and access details—parking, keys, pets, or priorities— so your cleaner is prepared and on time.

3

The clean

Your Vanza professional arrives fully equipped and follows a structured checklist for your booking—thorough, consistent, and respectful of your space.

4

Manager quality review

Your operations manager reviews before-and-after photos from the visit to confirm the clean has been completed to our high standard—only then do we sign off the job.

5

After the visit

A quick walkthrough where it helps, then payment as agreed. What you booked is what you pay—no hidden costs.

6

Aftercare & support

Something not quite right? Tell us within 24 hours and we’ll put it right. Your feedback keeps every clean improving.
