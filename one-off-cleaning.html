Service Overview

Our One-Off Cleaning service is designed for those moments when you need a professional deep clean without committing to a regular schedule. Whether you're preparing for a special event, recovering from a busy period, or simply want to refresh your home, we're here to help.

This flexible service allows you to choose exactly what you need. Our experienced cleaners will tackle your priorities, from kitchen deep cleaning to bathroom sanitization, living room organization to bedroom freshening. Every one-off clean is customized to your specific requirements and schedule.

Unless we agree otherwise, suitable cleaning supplies and equipment should be available at your property. If you would like us to provide supplies and/or equipment, please tell us in advance—ideally when you book—so we can confirm the arrangement. Our team works efficiently to deliver exceptional results, leaving your space spotless and inviting without disrupting your day.

Perfect for busy professionals, parents, or anyone who needs occasional professional cleaning support. Book as frequently or infrequently as you need, with no long-term commitment required.

What's Included

Kitchen Cleaning

  • Clean & sanitize all surfaces
  • Wipe down appliance exteriors
  • Clean hob, sink & taps
  • Wipe cabinet fronts & handles
  • Clean tiles & backsplash
  • Empty bins & replace liners
  • Mop & vacuum floors
  • Clean light switches & sockets

Bathroom Cleaning

  • Scrub & sanitize toilet
  • Clean shower/bath & tiles
  • Polish mirrors & glass
  • Clean sink, taps & fixtures
  • Remove limescale buildup
  • Wipe down surfaces & shelves
  • Empty bins & replace liners
  • Mop floors thoroughly

Living Areas

  • Dust all surfaces & furniture
  • Vacuum sofas & cushions
  • Clean TV screens & electronics
  • Wipe shelves & decorations
  • Clean mirrors & picture frames
  • Vacuum & mop all floors
  • Clean skirting boards
  • Organize & tidy visible areas

Bedrooms

  • Change bed linens (if provided)
  • Dust all furniture surfaces
  • Wipe wardrobes & drawers
  • Clean mirrors & windows
  • Vacuum under beds
  • Organize visible clutter
  • Clean light fixtures
  • Vacuum & mop floors
